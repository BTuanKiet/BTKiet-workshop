# Sharing and Feedback

Participating in the First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ) internship program has been a valuable experience that allowed me to apply my academic knowledge in a practical environment while gaining exposure to Cloud Computing and AWS technologies.

Throughout the internship, I had the opportunity to research AWS services, build cloud-based solutions, develop technical documentation, and contribute to workshop and blog content. These experiences helped me strengthen both my technical knowledge and professional skills.

Beyond the technical aspects, the internship also improved my ability to learn independently, collaborate with mentors, and adapt to new technologies. The guidance and support from mentors encouraged me to think critically, solve problems independently, and continuously improve myself.

Overall, I am grateful for the opportunity to participate in this program. It has provided valuable practical experience and strengthened my confidence in pursuing a career in Cloud Computing.

---

## Overall Feedback

### Working Environment

The learning environment was friendly, professional, and supportive. Mentors and other members were always willing to share their knowledge and provide guidance whenever needed, creating a positive atmosphere for learning and personal development.

### Mentor Support

The mentors provided clear explanations and constructive feedback throughout the internship. Rather than simply giving answers, they encouraged independent thinking and guided me toward finding appropriate solutions, which greatly improved my learning experience.

### Learning Experience

The internship provided opportunities to explore AWS services, cloud architecture, and technical documentation through practical tasks. These activities helped bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world applications.

### Personal Growth

Besides improving my technical knowledge, I also developed better communication, teamwork, self-learning, and problem-solving skills. These experiences have become an important foundation for my future career.

---

## Suggestions

- Organize learning content with clear priorities to make it easier for interns to follow.
- Continue updating the learning content to keep pace with technological advancements.
- Maintain and further enhance the quality of the program for future internship cohorts.

---

## Closing Remarks

I sincerely appreciate the dedication and support of the mentors and the FCAJ team throughout the internship. This experience has been meaningful and has inspired me to continue learning, improving my skills, and pursuing a career in Cloud Computing.
