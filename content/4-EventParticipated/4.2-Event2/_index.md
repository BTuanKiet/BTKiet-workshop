# Summary Report: "FCAJ Community Day - June 27th"

### Event information

- **Event name:** FCAJ Community Day - June 27th
- **Date:** June 27, 2026
- **Location:** 36th Floors, Bitexco Tower, Ho Chi Minh City
- **Role:** Attendee

### Event objectives

- Share practical enterprise experiences and perspectives on applying **AI** and **Cloud**.
- Introduce advanced AI solutions such as **Deep Response Engine**, **Voice Agents**, and **DevOps Agent** for operational optimization.
- Analyze how **Amazon Q** can modernize HR and enterprise management workflows.
- Explain security architecture considerations when connecting AI to internal enterprise data through **MCP (Model Context Protocol)**.

### Key highlights

#### Deep Response Engine and operations automation

- The event introduced a shift from **alert-driven** systems to **action-driven** systems.
- Autonomous Incident Response can automatically inspect and resolve incidents, helping reduce downtime for enterprises.
- This approach shortens the time needed to detect and recover from operational issues.

#### Building Voice Agents

- Voice Agents provide AI voice assistants that can communicate more naturally with users.
- Vietnamese is a lower-resource language, so latency, tone, and context require careful model and pipeline design.
- The workflow includes speech-to-text (**STT**), processing through an **LLM**, and voice response through text-to-speech (**TTS**).
- Gender detection and interruption handling make the conversation feel closer to real human interaction.

#### AWS DevOps Agent

- DevOps Agent uses AI to analyze logs and traces automatically, helping reduce **MTTD** and **MTTR**.
- A **Multi-Agent** architecture supports multi-cloud and hybrid environments by assigning specialized tasks to different agents.
- This solution is useful for large systems that require fast incident response.

#### HR management with Amazon Q

- Amazon Q can help solve HR challenges such as manual CV screening, time-consuming review, and subjective evaluation.
- It can automate recruitment steps such as creating job descriptions, filtering candidates, scheduling interviews, and sending emails.
- Standardized evaluation criteria make the HR process more transparent and consistent.

#### Securing MCP connectivity with Amazon Q

- Private connectivity through **VPC Connection** helps keep enterprise data away from the public internet when working with AI.
- **MCP Server** extends AI capabilities by allowing secure access to internal tools such as Zalo, Slack, or private data systems.
- This is important when introducing AI into environments with strict security requirements.

### Key takeaways

- **AI supports humans rather than fully replacing them:** In critical infrastructure, people still need to review and make final decisions.
- **Clean data is the foundation for AI:** Reliable logs, metrics, and observability data help AI produce more accurate analysis.
- Understood the difference between **Single Agent** and **Multi-Agent** designs, especially for cost, context, and task scope.
- Learned how to optimize AI operating costs through private connectivity, task decomposition, and token management.

### Applying to work

- Use **Amazon Q** to build a standardized personnel evaluation framework and reduce subjective factors in recruitment.
- Develop a **DevOps Agent** concept to support automated root-cause analysis during system incidents.
- Build a **Voice Bot** for customer support that can understand Vietnamese user context and sentiment.
- Set up **VPC Private Connectivity** for AI projects that must comply with strict enterprise security requirements.

### Event experience

- The event was professionally organized on the 26th and 36th floors of Bitexco Tower with many attendees.
- The **live demos** of Voice Agent and Autonomous Incident Response gave a practical view of how AI can support real operations.
- I had the opportunity to connect with CEOs, Solution Architects, and experts to discuss cost, security, and implementation roadmaps.

### Lessons learned

- **Execution over thinking:** Instead of spending too much time only planning, it is better to start with small POCs/MVPs to validate ideas.
- **AI amplifies skills:** People who know how to use AI well will have a strong advantage in today's labor market.
- **Security is the top priority:** When AI is introduced into an enterprise, internal data protection must be considered from the architecture design stage.
